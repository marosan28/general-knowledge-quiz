Rulebook

  1. Click on the start button on the front page
  2. Enter Username in the field where it says 'username' and press next button.
  3. You will be presented with a question and 4 possible answers.
  4. Click on the answer of your choice and check your answer by clicking on the next button.
  5. The score will be changing in the top left, below the title.
  6. Enjoy the game!
